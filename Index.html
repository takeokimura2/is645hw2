Easy Way Out

Mobile Transit App

Easy Way Out is a startup company to provide technological solutions for public transit to municipalities across the United State.
It was established by a graduate school student of Califronia State University Long Beach in 2021.
Easy Way Out is based in Cypress,California.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide technological solutions of public transit to municipalities and improve public transituser satisfaction.

Easy Way Out App

  • Easy Route Search
  • Real-Time Map
  • Mobile Payment
  • Single Account Across Various Municipal Public Transits
  • Automatc Reload of Rider Balance
  • Absolutely Free and No Deposit Required!

Why Easy Way Out?

  1. Increase Rider Satisfaction
  2. Attract Regular and One-time Riders
  3. Management of Rider Fare Collection
  4. Real-time Tracking of Vehicles by GPS
  5. Rider Data Collection and Analysis
  6. Assisting Public Transit Planning and Development
  7. Marketing Consultation
